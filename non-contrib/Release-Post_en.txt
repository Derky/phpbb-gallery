[*][b]from 0.4.0-RC3 to 0.4.0[/b]:

[*][b]from 0.3.x to 0.4.0-RC1[/b]:
	Because of a complete change on the permissions-system you need to set all permissions new, when you update from previous versions (than 0.4.x), or convert from Smartor's Album MOD.
